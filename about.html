My name is Xiangfeng(Allen) Zhu. I am a junior at University of Michigan, majoring in Computer Science. My research interests include Networking, Cloud computing and Distributed systems. Before I transferred to Michigan, I was a student and undergraduate researcher at UC Santa Cruz, where I was fortunate to be advised by Prof. Peter Alvaro and Prof. J.J. Garcia-Luna-Aceves.

I'm currently working under Prof. Mosharaf Chowdhury and his student Fan Lai on a project related to geo-distributed data processing.

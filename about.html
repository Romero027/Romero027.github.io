My name is Xiangfeng(Allen) Zhu. I am an undergraduate student at University of Michigan, majoring in Computer Science. I'm currently working under Prof. Mosharaf Chowdhury and Prof. Harsha V. Madhyastha projects related to Big data/ML systems. Before I transferred to Michigan, I was a student and undergraduate researcher at UC Santa Cruz, where I was fortunate to be advised by Prof. Peter Alvaro and Prof. J.J. Garcia-Luna-Aceves.

My current research interests include Cloud computing, Distributed systems, Systems for ML and Federated Learning.

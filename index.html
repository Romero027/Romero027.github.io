About:

I'm a first year Ph.D. student at the Systems Lab, University of Washington, advised by Arvind Krishnamurthy and Ratul Mahajan. I am also working closely with Danyang Zhuo and Matthew Lentz.

Previously, I obtained my bachelor's degree (with honors) from University of Michigan, where I worked with Mosharaf Chowdhury at the SymbioticLab . I also worked with Harsha V. Madhyastha, Junchen Jiang, and Peter Alvaro

My research interests are broadly in systems and networking. My recent works have focused on building systems for emerging large-scale workloads such as big data analytics and machine learning.

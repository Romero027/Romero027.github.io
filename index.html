About:

I'm a third year Ph.D. student at the Systems Lab and FOCI Center, University of Washington, advised by Arvind Krishnamurthy and Ratul Mahajan. I am also working closely with Danyang Zhuo.

Previously, I obtained my bachelor's degree (with honors) from University of Michigan, where I was advised by Mosharaf Chowdhury at the SymbioticLab. I also worked with Matthew Lentz, Harsha V. Madhyastha, Junchen Jiang, and Peter Alvaro.

My research interests are broadly in systems and networking. My recent works focus on microservices, service mesh, and application-level networking.
